Our Story

A Full-Service
Construction Partner

At TEXCO Construction, we believe every project starts with a commitment to quality, integrity, and doing the job right. We proudly serve property owners, ranchers, farmers, and businesses throughout Brazoria County and the surrounding areas with dependable construction services designed to meet the needs of Texas landowners.

From custom post-frame buildings and red iron buildings to fencing, concrete work, and site preparation, our team is dedicated to delivering durable solutions that provide lasting value. Whether you're developing agricultural property, improving a ranch, expanding a business, or building a new structure from the ground up, TEXCO Construction has the experience and capabilities to help bring your vision to life.
